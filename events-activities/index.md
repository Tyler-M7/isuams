Whether it’s ice skating under the evening skyline in Des Moines, decorating delicious cookies at Christmas, or becoming an intrepid adventurer while in the middle of a corn maze, the ISU AMS has social events that anyone can enjoy! If you’re looking for a friendly and inviting atmosphere and for an award-winning student organization to be a part of, join us for any one of our many events taking place this year.
