LAS Council - Liz White

    -At the last LAS Council meeting they talked about possible new student library fees in the range of $25 per person per semester.  Printing costs and journal subscription rates continue to go up, and our library's ranking has been slipping (it used to be rated one of the top research libraries in the country).  The library fees will only be put in place if there is student support.  Watch your email for a survey about this.

    -GSB has had a large number of senators drop out and many will be leaving at the end of the year.  If you are interested in running for GSB, talk to Liz.

    -The LAS council is participating in the VEISHEA parade.  If you are interested in representing meteorology in the parade, talk to Liz.

    -The Got Ignorance? campaign will have a bake sale on campus in the near future.  You will receive a free baked good if you talk to them and tell them you support their message!
